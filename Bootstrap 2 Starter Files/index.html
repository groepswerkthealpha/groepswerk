What is BoostBoard?

The BoostBoard is an electric longboard. It has a big battery that can run you for about 20km. It also has a smart remote with an eco switch, so the speed is limited when necessary. The eco-mode will automatically turn on when the battery is lower than 30%. Motor temperatures are also shown on the display.

Premium Materials

The BoostBoard is built with premium materials that are meant to last long. The motor is also 95% closed so barely any dirt can come in.

Warranty included

The BoostBoard also comes with 3 year warranty, which is higher than the 2 years of many other European companies.

Engineered with care

All components have been carefully tested and measured against faulty components. If you experience a bug, make sure to let us know what, and we'll work on it. We have also developped our product with safety first. This means that we've tested the batteries against shot circuit, the braking system makes sure that you dont shoot forward whenever you stop pressing the brake.
