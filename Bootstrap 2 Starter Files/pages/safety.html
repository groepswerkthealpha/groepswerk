Safety First!

The boostboard is protected with software and hardware
That is because we think that safety is important with our products.
It is protected against low battery, temperature, overcurrent, disconnections.
If you found a safety problem with our product, please contact us,
and we will take action.
And remember to love your brain and put a helmet on if you wanna go fast!

Low battery

The boostboard has a component that will make sure the batteries don't
undercharge or overcharge, and will balance the battery voltage.
Softwarematically, we've made sure that no current goes to
the motor when the voltage is too low.

Temperature

We have placed a temperature sensor inside of the motor.
this will make the board go to eco mode, if it is on the high side
of things. If the temps are just too high, the board will shut down.
If we don't do this, the spool's wires will short.
thank us later.

Overcurrent

There are 2 components that block overcurrents. These are the Vesc and the BMS.
First, the BMS will block too high currents in a few milliseconds.
Then the batteries won't output power to the VESC for about a day.
The Vesc will make sure the motor doesnt draw too much current.
This will make the batteries stay intact.

Connection problems

Whenever the remote disconnects from the board, the
motor will stop drawing current.
this will keep you safer then when it would
keep going at a certain speed for about 10 seconds,
Which would be enough to wound you.
